Your SUSE account gives you access to the machines in the internal SUSE network with your centrally shared home directory via NIS. It also gives you access to your @suse.de email address.

Your home directory ('/suse/<suse_username>') is on a central storage system. It's regularly backed up and you can access the backup in the magic directory code ~/.snapshotNote that space on the storage system is limited, so better not store your collection of movies or ISOs in your home directory.

You get your username and initial password from your team lead. Make sure to change the password after first login.
